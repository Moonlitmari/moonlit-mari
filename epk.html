Who is Mari

A creator who shows up as herself

Mari is a cosplay creator and live event professional based in Atlanta, GA. Outside of cosplay, she has a career in curating live events and festivals — which means she understands how events work, how to read a room, and how to make any appearance a success. She is flexible, professional, and genuinely fun to work with.

At conventions, Mari shines as a panel host, emcee, and brand ambassador — someone with the stage presence to command a room and the warmth to make every person in it feel welcome. She runs panels designed to spark inspiration and bring people together, not just fill a time slot.

For Mari, this is never about personal fame or individual success. It is about connecting the cosplay community to brands that share their values — and building something that lifts everyone up together. People are her passion. Community is her purpose.

Content pillars

Cosplay Builds & Tutorials

Con vlogs, packing guides, behind-the-scenes chaos, and wholesome community moments. Coverage of major Southeast cons including DragonCon and MomoCon.

Why certain characters matter, what cosplay teaches us about identity, and how the themes in anime and games connect to real life. Thoughtful, heartfelt, and always personal.
