Hi! I'm Mari — former theatre kid, con-going dreamer, and beginner/intermediate cosplayer who stumbled into this craft and never looked back. I'm not a professional. I make mistakes on camera. I hot-glue my fingers together and laugh about it.

This space was built for anyone who's ever looked at a cosplay and thought "I could never do that." You can. You just need a starting point and a crew that roots for you. Consider this yours.
