The maker behind the magic

A place where you belong
exactly as you are

Hi! I'm Mari — former theatre kid, con-going dreamer, and beginner/intermediate cosplayer who stumbled into this craft and never looked back. I'm not a professional. I make mistakes on camera and off. I hot-glue my fingers, prick myself with the needles, and always seem to be reaching toward a seam ripper more than once during every project.

I want this space to be for anyone who's ever looked at a cosplay and thought "I could never do that." Quit lying to yourself, because you totally can. You just need a starting point and a crew that roots for you. Consider yourself a part of the crew, stardust! We are here to cheer you on every step of the journey.
