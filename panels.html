Guest appearances & convention panels

Panels & Appearances

One of my favourite parts of con culture is the shared space of a panel room — where strangers who love the same things become instant friends. I'd love to bring that energy to your event.

What I talk about

Panel offerings

So You Want to Start Cosplaying?

The welcoming front door of the convention experience. Budget cosplay, closet cosplay, wig basics, first con survival tips, and genuine confidence-building for shy beginners. No gatekeeping, no intimidation — just practical tips you can act on the same day.

45–60 min

The Price is Fright

Cosplay meets The Price is Right — contestants guess the costs of cosplay builds, con supplies, and prop materials. Dramatic reveals, sound effects, and maximum audience chaos energy.

50–60 min

The Heart of Fantasy: Why Escapism Matters

A heartfelt, emotional discussion about fantasy and fandom as genuine emotional need — not just entertainment. Why fictional worlds feel like home, how fandoms help people through grief and isolation, and the real community built through shared stories.

60–75 min

So You Wanna Be a Superfan?

A Who Wants to Be a Millionaire-style fandom trivia showstopper. One contestant, escalating difficulty, three lifelines, dramatic tension, and an audience that lives and dies with every answer. Prizes for every tier from Casual Fan to SUPERFAN.

60–75 min

Making Conventions Feel Magical Again

A community conversation about fandom burnout, gatekeeping, convention kindness, and how to bring the magic back together. Open discussion, genuine vulnerability, and a group moment to end on.

60–75 min

Characterization: Bringing Your Cosplay to Life

More than a costume — it's a performance. Drawing on her background as a former Disney Entertainment Cast Member, Mari shares tips and tricks for embodying a character: physicality, voice, energy, and how to create magical moments for the people you meet.

45–60 min
