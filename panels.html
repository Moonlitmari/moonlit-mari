Guest appearances & convention panels

Panels & Appearances

One of my favourite parts of con culture is the shared space of a panel room — where strangers who love the same things become instant friends. I'd love to bring that energy to your event.

What I talk about

Panel offerings

Beginner Cosplay: You Already Have Everything You Need

A warm, encouraging intro to the world of cosplay for anyone who's felt too intimidated to start. We cover mindset, materials, and the magic of just beginning.

60–90 min

Storytelling Through Costume: Character, Identity & Craft

A deeper conversation about why we cosplay, what our characters mean to us, and how putting on a costume can help us step into who we want to become.

60 min

Building Your Cosplay Community: Finding Your Crew

How to find your people online and offline, build a space that's drama-light and encouragement-heavy, and why community is the best part of this whole journey.

45–60 min

Convention First Timer: Everything I Wish I'd Known

Practical tips, social wisdom, and heartfelt encouragement for anyone heading to their first con. Packing, panels, photos, and being kind to yourself throughout.

45 min

Progress Over Perfection: The Cosplay Mindset

A pep talk panel for anyone who's compared themselves, felt "not good enough," or almost quit. We celebrate the messy middle and the courage it takes to show up.

60 min

Content Creation for Cosplayers: Sharing Your Journey Online

A beginner-friendly guide to documenting your builds, growing a genuine audience, and creating content that feels like *you* — not just an algorithm.

60–75 min
